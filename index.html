About Me

I'm a QA Engineer with strong foundation in software testing methodologies, test case design, and quality validation gained through TripleTen QA Bootcamp. My unique background combines analytical thinking from my Philosophy degree with operational experience managing critical web-based systems in hospitality environments.

Throughout my QA training, I've developed expertise in manual testing, API testing with Postman, cross-browser validation, and bug reporting using industry-standard tools like JIRA. I approach testing with a user-centric mindset, thinking critically about how software failures impact end-users and business operations.

My experience coordinating quality processes across educational and hospitality settings has taught me the importance of attention to detail, clear communication with stakeholders, and systematic problem-solving—all essential skills for effective quality assurance.
